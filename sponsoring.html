Sponsoring NixCon 2018

NixCon 2018 is a volunteer and community conference that depends on your sponsorship to happen.

We offer Gold, Silver, Lanyard and Contributor sponsor packages. If you have other ideas how you want to sponsor the event, please talk to us. We are happy to create a new sponsor package just for you.

In order to confirm your sponsorship, please send the following information to orgateam (at) nixcon2018.org:
Contact person, Contact person email, Company name, Company address, Company VAT number, Sponsorship package type and a logo for website and posters.
You will receive an invoice including VAT, if applicable. Invoices will be handled by our conference partner, innoQ.

Silver Sponsor ‐ 250 € (excl. VAT)

Benefits:

  • Logo on the nixcon2018.org website
  • 1 seat reserved

Gold Sponsor ‐ 1000 € (excl. VAT)

Benefits:

  • Logo on the nixcon2018.org website
  • 50 word description on the website
  • 4 seats reserved
  • Flyer/swag on shared table

Contributor and Diversity Sponsor ‐ 1000 € (excl. VAT)

We aim to help people who make significant contributions to Nix and people of underrepresented groups in tech who can't afford travelling to attend.


Benefits:

  • Logo on the nixcon2018.org website
  • 50 word description on the website
  • 4 seats reserved
  • Flyer/swag on shared table

Lanyard Sponsor ‐ 500 € (excl. VAT)

You sponsor the lanyard for all attendees. We will use any lanyard you provide. We need around 140 lanyards from you to make sure everybody gets one.


Benefits:

  • Logo on the nixcon2018.org website
  • 2 seats reserved
  • Flyer/swag on shared table
